General Guidelines


1. Language. The quizzes are all in English.

2. Quiz Participation, you will be asked to submit each question, you will not be able to change your selections.

3. Cheating. Anyone suspected by an official of cheating will be disqualified. You must not use any mobile device during a quiz competition.

4. Challenges. If at any time you think your answer should be accepted, then you must complete a challenge slip.

5. Time will be recorded for complete quiz.

6. A Test Consists of 10 Multiple Choice Question, Each question have 4 Choices

7. Every Question must be answered. Navigate between the questions is not allowed.

8. Quiz must be submitted or else score wil not considered.
